FAQ & QR Code Longevity

Why do some QR codes stop working?

Some QR codes (especially those generated by paid services or for URLs that change or expire) become invalid because the destination is deleted, the code is part of a short-lived campaign, or the provider removes hosting. Dynamic QR codes can also expire if the owner revokes, deletes, or stops paying for the link[web:5].

What else can QR codes store?

QR codes can store URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, Wi-Fi credentials, and even vCards for contact sharing[web:3][web:4].
